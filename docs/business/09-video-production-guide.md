# Video Production Guide: First Two Videos

> Print this or keep it open on your laptop while filming.
> These two videos are your launch pair: Video 1 grabs attention. Video 2 makes them follow.

---

## Video 1: "Hice una app que me cuenta mis calorias"

### The Concept

Your **strongest first video**. The AI nutrition feature is the most visually impressive, demo-able, and unique thing your app does. Nobody else has this in a free calisthenics app. The video shows the problem (other apps are expensive and annoying) and the solution (yours, free, instant).

### Pre-Production Checklist

- [ ] Open the app on your phone, make sure AI nutrition is working
- [ ] Prepare a real meal to demo (something visual — plate with food on a table)
- [ ] Have the competitor apps ready to show their pricing (screenshots or open their websites)
- [ ] Charge your phone
- [ ] Clean your notification bar (turn on Do Not Disturb)
- [ ] Pick a spot at home with decent lighting (face a window)
- [ ] Wear a plain dark t-shirt (no logos that distract)

### Shot List (Film in This Order)

**SHOT 1 — The Frustration (talking head)**
- Camera: Front camera, eye level, you filling most of the frame
- Where: At home, sitting at desk or standing
- Duration: Film 10 seconds, you'll use 3-4
- What you say: "Queria trackear mi nutricion pero todas las apps cuestan 10 dolares al mes... por escanear un codigo de barras."
- Face: Slight annoyance/disbelief. Like telling a friend something ridiculous.
- Film it 3 times. Pick the most natural one.

**SHOT 2 — The Competitor Prices (screen recording)**
- Camera: Screen recorder on your phone
- What to show: Open MyFitnessPal or Yazio or FatSecret premium pricing page. Scroll slowly past the price. $9.99/month, $12.99/month, etc.
- Duration: Film 5-6 seconds of scrolling
- Alternative: Google "apps para contar calorias precio" and show the results

**SHOT 3 — The Transition (talking head)**
- Same setup as Shot 1
- What you say: "Asi que hice la mia." (casual, matter-of-fact, slight smile)
- Duration: 2 seconds
- This is the pivot moment. Keep it short and confident.

**SHOT 4 — The Demo (screen recording)**
- Camera: Screen recorder
- What to do (SLOWLY — people need to read the screen):
  1. Open your app, go to nutrition section
  2. Tap to add a meal
  3. Type: "2 huevos revueltos con tostada y cafe con leche" (or whatever you're actually eating)
  4. Wait for AI to process
  5. Show the result: calories, protein, carbs, fat
  6. Show it adding to your daily total
- Duration: Film the full flow (maybe 15-20 seconds), you'll cut it to 8-10
- Important: Tap slowly. Pause on the result for 2 seconds so people can read it.

**SHOT 5 — The Real Meal (optional but strong)**
- Camera: Back camera
- What to film: The actual plate of food you typed in. Quick 2-second shot.
- Why: Proves it's real. "That's the actual food, and those are the actual macros."

**SHOT 6 — The Reveal (talking head)**
- Same setup as Shot 1
- What you say: "Y es gratis. De verdad. Cero pesos."
- Face: Direct to camera, confident but not arrogant
- Duration: 3 seconds

**SHOT 7 — The App (screen recording)**
- Quick scroll through other features: workout program, calendar heatmap, progress chart
- Duration: 3-4 seconds, fast cuts
- Purpose: Show it's not JUST nutrition — it's a complete app

           Text overlay: "Las apps de nutricion cuestan $10+/mes"

           Text overlay: "$9.99/mes" or circle the price in red

0:05-0:07  SHOT 3 (talking head, "asi que hice la mia")
           Text overlay: "Asi que hice la mia"

           Text overlay at start: "Solo escribe lo que comiste"
           Text overlay on result: show the macros visually

           No text needed, or small "La comida real"

           Text overlay: "GRATIS" in big lime green text

           Text overlay: "Link en bio"

```

### Audio

- Option A: Your voice the whole way through (best for connection)
- Option B: Trending audio track underneath + text overlays only (easier to make, broader reach via trending sound)
- Option C: Mix — your voice for hooks and transition, music for the demo section

### Caption (Text Below the Video)

```

Asi que construi la mia — con IA que entiende lo que comes en espanol.

- [ ] Fix any caption errors manually

## Video 2: "Hice una app de calistenia por que todas son malas o caras"

### The Concept

Your **origin story**. Longer, more emotional, positions you as the underdog. This video is about YOU, not just the app. People will follow you because of this story. They'll download the app because of Video 1 (nutrition). Together, these two videos are your launch pair.

### Pre-Production Checklist

- [ ] Find screenshots of competitor apps and their pricing (Freeletics $12.99, Thenx $9.99, Hevy Pro $9.99)
- [ ] Have your app ready to show on your phone
- [ ] If you have any old workout notes (Notes app, paper, spreadsheet) — photograph them
- [ ] Pick a park where you train for the outdoor shots
- [ ] Bring a tripod or something to lean your phone against at the park

### Shot List (Film in This Order)

**AT HOME — Film these first:**

**SHOT 1 — The Hook (talking head)**
- What you say: "Probe todas las apps de calistenia. Todas. Y ninguna servia."
- Face: Genuine frustration. Like you're venting to a friend.
- Film 3 takes.

**SHOT 2 — The Problem (screen recording)**
- Show 2-3 competitor apps or their app store listings
- Show their pricing: Freeletics $12.99/mes, Thenx $9.99/mes
- Scroll through one of them briefly to show generic/bad UX
- Duration: 5-6 seconds

**SHOT 3 — The Old Way (screen recording or photo)**
- Show a screenshot of your old Notes app where you used to write workouts
- OR show a messy spreadsheet
- OR just describe it: "Anotaba todo en Notas como un cavernicola"
- Duration: 3 seconds

**SHOT 4 — The Decision (talking head)**
- What you say: "Soy programador. Asi que me hice la mia."
- Slight smile. This is the turning point of the story.
- Duration: 3 seconds

**SHOT 5 — The Code (b-roll)**
- Screen record your code editor with the actual codebase open
- Scroll through files, show the terminal, show a commit
- Duration: Film 10 seconds, use 3-4
- This establishes credibility — you actually built this

**SHOT 6 — The App Today (screen recording)**
- This is the big reveal. Show the app in its current polished state.
- Flow: Dashboard → open a workout → show exercises → complete a set → show progress chart → show calendar heatmap → show share card
- Film slowly. Each screen 2-3 seconds.
- Duration: Film 20 seconds, cut to 10-12

**SHOT 7 — Features Montage (screen recording)**
- Quick cuts of features, 1-2 seconds each:
  - AI nutrition (type a meal, get macros)
  - Sleep tracking chart
  - Challenge leaderboard
  - Streak milestone celebration
  - Exercise library scrolling
- Duration: 6-8 seconds total

**SHOT 8 — The Stats (talking head)**
- What you say: "150 ejercicios. 4 fases de progresion. Nutricion con IA. Retos con amigos. Seguimiento de sueno. Todo gratis."
- Say this with pride but not arrogance. Like listing what you cooked for a dinner party.
- Duration: 5-6 seconds

**AT THE PARK — Film these second:**

**SHOT 9 — You Training (exercise footage)**
- Tripod on the ground, back camera
- Film 3-4 exercises from your program: pull-ups, dips, push-ups, maybe a skill move
- 3-4 seconds per exercise
- Keep the phone with the app visible nearby if possible (on the ground showing today's workout)

**SHOT 10 — The Closing (talking head, at the park)**
- What you say: "La hice para mi. Ahora la uso todos los dias. Y es gratis para ti tambien."
- Film at the park, slightly sweaty from training. Authentic.
- Duration: 4-5 seconds

### Editing Plan (CapCut)

This video is longer — aim for 35-45 seconds.

```
Timeline:

0:00-0:03  SHOT 1 (hook — "probe todas las apps")
           Text: "Probe TODAS las apps de calistenia"

0:03-0:06  SHOT 2 (competitor prices scrolling)
           Text: "$12.99/mes" "$9.99/mes" (flash the prices)

0:06-0:08  SHOT 3 (old Notes app / spreadsheet)
           Text: "Antes anotaba todo asi..."

0:08-0:11  SHOT 4 (talking head — "soy programador")
           Text: "Soy programador. Asi que..."

0:11-0:14  SHOT 5 (code editor scrolling)
           Text: "6 meses despues..."

0:14-0:24  SHOT 6 (app reveal — big moment)
           Music drop or beat change here
           Text: "200+ archivos. 41 paginas. Hecho solo."
           Show each screen with brief label

0:24-0:30  SHOT 7 (features montage, fast cuts)
           Text labels on each: "IA" "Sueno" "Retos" "Racha" "150+ ejercicios"

0:30-0:36  SHOT 9 (training at the park, intercut)
           Text: "Sin gimnasio. Sin pesas."

0:36-0:41  SHOT 10 (closing at the park)
           Text: "Gratis. De verdad. Link en bio."
           OR your voice saying it

Total: ~40 seconds
```

### Audio

- Best option: Your voice throughout (this is a story — your voice carries emotion)
- Music: Something that builds. Calm/quiet in the frustration section (shots 1-3), then a subtle beat drop when you reveal the app (shot 6). CapCut has free tracks that work.
- The beat change at the reveal is important — it signals "here comes the good part"

## Recording Day Plan

Film both videos in one session:

```
AT HOME (1 hour):
  □ Change into plain dark t-shirt
  □ Set up phone on tripod at eye level, face a window
  □ Film all talking head shots for BOTH videos:
      Video 1: shots 1, 3, 6
      Video 2: shots 1, 4, 8
  □ Film screen recordings for BOTH videos:
      - Competitor pricing pages (reuse across both)
      - Old Notes app screenshots
      - AI nutrition demo (prepare real food)
      - Full app walkthrough
      - Features montage
      - Code editor scrolling

AT THE PARK (45 min):
  □ Set up tripod
  □ Film 4-5 exercises from your program
  □ Film the closing talking head (slightly sweaty = authentic)
  □ Film ambient shots (bars, park, walking there)
  □ Film yourself opening the app at the park, checking today's workout

EDITING (1 hour):
  □ Open CapCut
  □ Edit Video 1 first (shorter, simpler, ~22 seconds)
  □ Edit Video 2 second (longer, more cuts, ~40 seconds)
  □ Add text overlays, auto-captions, music
  □ Export both
  □ Write captions for both
```
