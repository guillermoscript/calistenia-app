# Marketing Funnel (BasementBeast-Style)

## How BasementBeast Does It

BasementBeast follows a classic direct-response fitness marketing playbook:

1. **Facebook/Instagram video ad** — Transformation story, emotional hook
2. **Content landing page** — Educates, builds trust, soft-sells
3. **Product page** — Clear offer with urgency
4. **Purchase** — Bundle/stack with limited-time pricing

They don't send ad traffic to their homepage. They send it to **dedicated funnel pages** optimized for one action.

## The Funnel Landing Page (For Paid Ads)

This is NOT your main landing page. This is a separate, focused page that ad traffic goes to. One message, one CTA, zero distractions.

### Structure:

**1. Hero (above the fold)**
- Headline: "Entrena calistenia con un plan real" or "Tu programa de calistenia personalizado"
- Subhead: Problem-focused — "Sin plan, sin progreso. Deja de improvisar."
- Visual: App screenshot showing a workout session in progress OR a before/after
- Single CTA button: "Empieza tu prueba gratis de 7 dias"
- No navigation menu. No footer links. Nothing that takes them away.

**2. Social proof (just below fold)**
- 3-4 testimonials from real users with their progress
- "2,000+ atletas ya entrenan con nosotros" (when you have the numbers)
- Star rating if you collect reviews

**3. The problem (emotional)**
- "Entrenas sin un plan estructurado"
- "No sabes si estas progresando"
- "Pruebas rutinas de YouTube que no llevan a nada"
- This should feel like you're describing THEIR situation

**4. The solution (3 key benefits, not features)**
- "Programa de 6 meses con periodizacion real" → Screenshot of program view
- "Mide tu progreso con datos, no con sensaciones" → Screenshot of analytics
- "Nutricion con IA que se adapta a tus macros" → Screenshot of meal plan

**5. How it works (3 steps)**
- Step 1: "Crea tu cuenta en 30 segundos"
- Step 2: "Elige tu programa y empieza hoy"
- Step 3: "Registra tus entrenos y ve tu progreso"

**6. Feature grid (quick scan)**
- 157 ejercicios con video
- GPS para cardio
- Escaneo de codigo de barras
- Funciona sin internet
- Retos con amigos
- Seguimiento de sueno
- Salud lumbar (ejercicios especializados)
- Disponible en espanol e ingles

**7. Final CTA**
- "Empieza gratis — tu primer entrenamiento te espera"
- Same button as hero
- Add urgency if applicable: "Primeros 500 usuarios: prueba extendida de 14 dias"

**8. FAQ (trust builders)**
- "Es realmente gratis para empezar?" → Si, 7 dias completos sin pagar nada
- "Puedo cancelar cuando quiera?" → Si, sin preguntas
- "Funciona en mi telefono?" → Si, cualquier telefono con navegador

## Facebook/Instagram Ad Strategy

### When to Start Ads

**NOT until you have:**
- Payment working (Stripe integrated)
- The funnel landing page live
- At least 5 testimonials from real users
- A clear free-to-paid conversion rate from organic traffic (even if small)

Realistically: Month 5-6.

### Ad Creative Types (Ranked by Expected Performance)

**1. Screen Recording + Voiceover (Highest ROI)**
- Record yourself using the app: starting a workout, logging sets, seeing progress
- Voiceover: "Llevo 3 meses entrenando calistenia con esta app y estos son mis resultados"
- Show the progress charts, the streak, the session history
- 15-30 seconds, vertical format
- This works because it's authentic and shows the actual product

**2. Before/After Transformation**
- Split screen: before photos vs after photos
- Text overlay: "12 semanas de calistenia"
- End card: "Descarga la app gratis"
- Note: Only use real transformations. Fake ones get flagged and destroy trust.

**3. Problem-Solution Hook**
- Open with: "Estas haciendo flexiones sin saber si estas mejorando?"
- Show the problem (training without tracking)
- Show the solution (app logging, charts going up)
- CTA: "Prueba 7 dias gratis"

**4. Tutorial Clip + App Mention**
- Teach something useful (a progression, a form tip)
- Naturally mention: "Esto es parte del programa que sigo en [App Name]"
- Softest sell, best for cold audiences

**5. Sleep + Recovery Angle**
- "Duermes mal? Tu entrenamiento lo nota"
- Show sleep tracking → correlate with workout performance
- Targets the health-conscious audience beyond just strength

**6. Lumbar Health / Rehab Hook**
- "5 ejercicios para el dolor de espalda baja"
- High-demand topic that targets an older demographic (30-50)
- Positions the app as health + strength, not just aesthetics

### Targeting

| Audience | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Core** | Males 18-35, interests: calisthenics, street workout, bodyweight training, bar brothers, Chris Heria, ThenX |
| **Broad fitness** | Males 18-35, interests: home workout, gym, fitness, no equipment workout |
| **Lookalike** | 1% lookalike of your registered users (once you have 100+) |
| **Retargeting** | Visited landing page but didn't sign up (install Meta Pixel) |
| **Countries (Spanish)** | Spain, Mexico, Colombia, Argentina, Chile, Peru |
| **Countries (English)** | US, UK, Canada, Australia — the app is now fully bilingual |
| **English audiences** | r/bodyweightfitness, r/calisthenics, English calisthenics YouTube communities |

### Budget Progression

| Month | Daily Budget | Monthly Total | Expected Signups | Cost per Signup |
|-------|-------------|---------------|-----------------|----------------|
| 5 | $10/day | $300 | 60-100 | $3-5 |
| 6 | $15/day | $450 | 100-150 | $3-4.50 |
| 7-8 | $15-20/day | $500-600 | 120-180 | $3-4 |
| 9-12 | $20-25/day | $600-750 | 150-250 | $3-4 |

**Kill rules:**
- If cost per signup > $7 after 3 days → kill the ad creative
- If cost per signup > $5 after 5 days → test new audience
- If signup-to-paid conversion < 2% → fix onboarding/trial, not ads

### The Retargeting Sequence

People who visit your landing page but don't sign up:

- **Day 1-3**: Show a different ad creative (social proof focused)
- **Day 4-7**: Show testimonial/transformation ad
- **Day 7-14**: "Todavia pensandolo? 7 dias gratis esperandote"
- **After 14 days**: Stop (they're not interested)

Retargeting budget: 20% of total ad spend. Retargeting converts 3-5x better than cold traffic.

## Metrics That Matter

### Funnel Conversion Rates (Targets)

| Stage | Target Rate | Red Flag |
|-------|------------|----------|
| Ad click-through rate | >1.5% | <0.8% |
| Landing → signup | >15% | <8% |
| Signup → complete onboarding | >60% | <40% |
| Signup → first workout | >40% | <20% |
| Trial → paid | >5% | <2% |
| Month 1 paid retention | >85% | <70% |
| Month 3 paid retention | >65% | <50% |

### The One Metric That Matters Most

**Trial-to-paid conversion rate.**

If this is >5%, scale ads. If this is <2%, stop spending and fix the product/onboarding. Everything else is secondary.

## Seasonal Opportunities

| Period | Opportunity | Action |
|--------|------------|--------|
| January | New Year's resolutions | Biggest signup month for fitness. Run your heaviest ad spend. Offer annual plan at 50% off. |
| March-April | "Summer body" motivation | "Empieza hoy, llega listo al verano" campaign |
| September | Back to routine after summer | "Vuelve a entrenar" re-engagement campaign |
| November (Black Friday) | Discount expectations | Lifetime deal at $89.99, annual at 50% off |

Plan your marketing calendar around these. January alone can represent 25-30% of your annual signups.
